About us

Using our service is very simple, you just add: audio, title, description and image. And we provide a link to a ready-made RSS feed. We also have a very user-friendly podcast manager.

Why we started doing this

The idea for this project started with the fact that there was no proper and cheap podcasting platform. That's why we started developing this product. Perhaps over time, we will face other problems and the price will increase. But for now...

The project is also under development. You can support it if you like what we're doing: Buy me a coffee
